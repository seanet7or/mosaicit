1. Installation And System Requirements

1.1 System Requirements

MosaicIt! runs under Windows XP, Vista, Windows 7 and Linux. Twenty MB of hard disk disk space are required for the installation. Your computer should have at least 256 MB of RAM. For your photo collection and the created mosaics, more hard disk drive will be needed.
Please note, that depending on the selected options you may need much more than 256 MB of RAM to render a big mosaic in a reasonable time. Rendering a photographic mosaic with 60 megapixels may need up to 300 MB of RAM and take up to 15 minutes on a fairly outdated AMD Athlon XP mobile 2600+.

1.2 Install MosaicIt!

Just run the setup.exe and follow the shown instructions to install MosaicIt! on your computer. At least 20 MB of free hard space are required for the program.

1.3 Uninstall MosaicIt!

There are two ways to remove MosaicIt! from your computer: Either you can select the "Uninstall" link in the programs start menu folder, or you open the Control Panel, select Add or Remove Programs and choose to remove MosaicIt!
